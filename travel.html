Travel

Photography and writing from travels across six continents. I try to document places with care — landscape, light, and the small details that tell a story.

Torres del Paine, Chile

Patagonia: At the End of the World

March 2024

Trekking through the dramatic landscapes of southern Patagonia — wind-battered plains, turquoise glacial lakes, and the iconic granite spires of Torres del Paine. Five days on the W Trek, condors overhead, and a puma crossing the path at dusk.

Kyoto & Nikko, Japan

Autumn Colours in Rural Japan

November 2023

Chasing koyo — the Japanese autumn colour season — through ancient temple gardens and forested mountain roads. The garden at Tofuku-ji before dawn; the Shinkyo Bridge framed in gold; the long blue twilight of Nikko's cedar forest.

Merzouga, Morocco

Into the Sahara

April 2023

A camel trek into the Erg Chebbi dunes, sleeping under an impossible sky of stars on the edge of the Sahara. Then north through the Atlas to the blue city of Chefchaouen, where every alleyway is washed in shades of cobalt and indigo.

Lofoten & Geiranger, Norway

The Norwegian Fjords in Winter

February 2022

Searching for the northern lights above snow-covered rorbuer fishing cabins in the Lofoten archipelago. After two cloudy nights, the sky cleared to reveal dancing curtains of emerald and magenta that filled the entire sky.
